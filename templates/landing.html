Features
Full market order lifecycle management

From order creation to fill tracking — EMOM covers every stage of your EVE market activity.

◇
Buy & Sell Orders
Track both buy and sell orders with independent views. Filter by type, status, region, character, or search by item name.
⪈
Margin Analysis
Record cost basis for sell orders. Get instant margin %, profit per unit, and unrealized profit totals. Sort and filter by profitability.
⏱
Expiry Monitoring
Dashboard alerts for orders expiring within 24 hours. Countdown timers per order. Sort orders by expiry to prioritize renewals.
◉
Fill Progress
Visual fill bars per order showing percentage complete. Track volume total vs remaining for all active orders at a glance.
⌛
Event History
Full audit trail: price changes, volume updates, status transitions, and manual notes — all timestamped to UTC (EVE time).
⪈
Performance Analytics
Breakdown by character, region, and item. Overall fill rates, fulfilled ISK, and top items by active value.
📊
Price Snapshots
Record price/volume snapshots at any time. Historical snapshot log per order to track price movements over time.
◇
Bulk Operations
Multi-select orders and bulk-update status. Purge expired/cancelled orders older than 30 days. Designed for high-volume traders.
